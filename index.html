♧ About Us

Magic Circus, your magical party partner! Magic Circus is a privately owned business that provides premium services of children parties and entertainment. Our company was founded in 2022 by Wei Zhang and Moana Belich. These two former university friends, who are also the professional magician and e-commerce expert decided to establish a business that can offer outstanding services in the child entertainment field. We are committed to helping our families with satisfied party solutions by providing comprehensive one-stop party planning, venue choosing, organizing & management, budget and cost advice, catering and more. Our vision is to create the worry-free party for all children and parents. Magic Circus, save your time to bring an unforgettable party to your kids!
